## 9. Verification: what to expect

Google picks the method; for a tour operator with no storefront, expect **video verification** to be the offered route. Prep before starting so it is one take:

- Do it at the launch site on a phone, signed into the profile's Google account
- Show: the street/area so the location is provable, the airboat itself (registration numbers FL-4661-RB visible), any branded gear or signage, and something proving management access, like the trailer, keys, or business paperwork
- Have LLC paperwork (SunBiz registration) handy in case documents are requested
- Takes up to 5 business days for Google to review after submission

Phone/SMS or email verification may also be offered. If so, take it, it is faster.
